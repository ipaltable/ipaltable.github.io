Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026 · PalTable, LLC

PalTable ("we," "us," or "our") operates the PalTable mobile application and related services that connect diners ("Pals") with restaurants hosting group dining events. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Account information: name, email, phone, password (stored securely by our auth provider), age, sex, zip code, profile photo, dining preferences, and dietary restrictions.
  • Restaurant information: business name, address, menu, event details, and payout details for restaurant accounts.
  • Booking and payment data: event selections, booking status, payment amounts, and transaction identifiers. Payment card numbers are processed by Stripe; we do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Location: with your permission, approximate device location to show nearby restaurants on the map. You can deny or revoke location access in iOS Settings.
  • Device and usage data: device tokens for push notifications (if enabled), app interactions, and diagnostic logs used to operate and improve the service.
  • Communications: messages you send through the app, support requests, and reports you submit about other users.

How we use information

  • Provide accounts, bookings, payments, restaurant tools, and customer support.
  • Send transactional notifications about bookings, cancellations, and refunds.
  • Prevent fraud, enforce our Terms, and comply with law.
  • Improve reliability, safety, and product features.

Third-party services

We use trusted processors including:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and file storage.
  • Stripe — payment processing and restaurant payouts (Stripe Connect).
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple (when enabled) and push notification delivery via APNs.

These providers process data under their own privacy policies and our agreements with them.

Sharing

We share limited information as needed to operate the service: restaurants see guest booking details for their events; other guests may see your first name and profile photo for shared tables as designed in the app. We do not sell your personal information.

Retention and deletion

We retain information while your account is active and as needed for legal, tax, and dispute purposes. You may delete your account in the app under Profile → Account Settings → Delete Account. Deletion removes your profile and associated data subject to legal retention requirements and active booking restrictions.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information. Contact us at support@paltable.com.

Children

PalTable is for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date and, where appropriate, in-app notice.
